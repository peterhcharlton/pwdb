Presentation on the Preliminary Pulse Wave Database

Publication

Publications Homepage

Summary

In this presentation we gave an overview of the Preliminary Pulse Wave Database. Details are given on the rationale for creating the database, its design, and its verification. The potential utility of the database is illustrated through a case study of pulse wave indices for assessing aortic stiffness.
